CLR-Bench: Evaluating Large Language Models in College-level Reasoning

About CLR-Bench

CLR-Bench is a comprehensive benchmark designed to evaluate large language models' reasoning abilities in college-level scenarios. This leaderboard tracks the performance of various LLMs across diverse academic domains, providing insights into their strengths and areas for improvement.
